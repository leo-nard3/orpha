Our orphanage is a safe and nurturing home for children who have been left without parental care. We provide a range of services and programs to support the physical, emotional, and educational needs of our residents.
Our Mission
Our mission is to provide a loving and supportive environment for children who have been orphaned or abandoned. We strive to meet their basic needs and help them grow into healthy, happy, and well-adjusted individuals.
Our History
Our orphanage was founded in 2008 by Rtd Rev.Tibuuza bernard, who was moved by the plight of children living on the streets and in poverty. Since then, we have grown to become one of the largest and most respected orphanages in the region. Currently, we are handling two hundred sixty one orphans and we look forward to improving their lives and better their future 'God being our helper'.
